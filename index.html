Summary

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines programmed to think and learn. Key concepts include: • Machine Learning: A subset of AI that enables systems to learn and improve from experience without explicit programming. • Neural Networks: Computational models inspired by the human brain, used for pattern recognition and classification. • Natural Language Processing (NLP): Technology that enables computers to understand, interpret, and respond to human language.
